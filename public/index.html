Welcome to Cafe Cougar at UH Sugarland! Meet our Head Chef and Owner

Chef Ronald Nguyen

Mr. Nguyen is considered the heart and soul of Cafe Cougar, his vision to create a cafeteria at the University of Houston Sugarland Campus offering healthy, affordable and delicious meals came to life in February of 2024. Since then he has had the privilege of being head chef and overseer of operations. He is an expert in diverse cuisine from all over the world and has even participated in various cooking workshops, learning a great deal from other famous chefs. This has allowed him to incorporate his knowledge into the kitchen at Cafe Cougar.

Born in Houston Texas, Mr. Nguyen grew up in a Vietnamese household. His father, who he quotes as his biggest inspiration in cooking, taught him everything he needed to know to cook at home. As he grew older he decided to take his love of cooking to the next level, enrolling in the Culinary Institute in America.

When Mr. Nguyen is not busy working at Cafe Cougar, he enjoys spending time with his wife and children. His favorite hobbies include travelling the world, birdwatching, and listening to classical music.
